"From a Management Trainee navigating the corridors of the ITC Group, to the artillery fields of the Indian Army, and finally — to the boardroom at Asahi India Glass Limited — Vikram Khanna's story is one of extraordinary discipline, relentless ambition, and 30 years of consistent value creation."

Joining AIS in 1996 as a Sales Officer, Vikram Khanna rose through every echelon of the organisation — leading Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, Commercial Operations, and as COO of Consumer Glass for over a decade. His military-honed precision and strategic clarity transformed divisions, opened markets, and built the consumer glass business from the ground up.

In April 2026, Asahi India Glass Limited recognised three decades of extraordinary service with the appointment that defines a legacy: President — AIS Consumer Glass Solutions LTD.
